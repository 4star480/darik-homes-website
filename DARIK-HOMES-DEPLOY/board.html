Board of Directors

Meet the visionary leaders guiding DARIK HOMES LTD's success

Dr. Babatunde Kazeem Adeleke

Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer

Corporate Member of the Nigeria Institute of Building (NIOB) and Registered Builder with the Council of Registered Builders of Nigeria (CORBON). He holds a Doctor of Philosophy Degree (Ph.D) in Construction Technology (Structures) from Ahmadu Bello University Zaria, with extensive experience in structural design & detailing, building construction, and project management.

Ph.D - Construction Technology (Structures)
M.Sc - Construction Technology (Structures)
B.Sc (Hons) - Building

Prof. Mustapha Muhammed Saad

Executive Director

Professor of Construction Management at Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria. He holds a Ph.D. in Construction Management from University of Witwatersrand, South Africa, and brings extensive leadership and corporate management skills in real estate and construction.

Ph.D - Construction Management
M.Sc - Construction Technology
B.Sc - Building

Mr. Ayoade Sakir Adeleke

Executive Director

Executive Director of DARIK HOMES LTD with extensive experience in real estate management and sustainability. He is a PhD candidate at Nasarawa State University, holds an M.Sc. in Mathematics from Ahmadu Bello University, Zaria, and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the University of Jos.

PhD Candidate - Nasarawa State University
M.Sc - Mathematics
B.Sc - Mathematics

Dr. Abdulwahab Lasisi

Director

A United Kingdom-based practicing medical doctor with a keen interest in entrepreneurship, particularly in real estate development. Currently serving as a Practicing GP (Family Medicine Consultant) in the United Kingdom. He holds an MBBS qualification in Medicine and Surgery from the University of Jos.

MBBS - Medicine and Surgery
MRCGP

Mr. Habib Abdulsalam

Company Secretary

Company Secretary of DARIK HOMES LTD, utilizing his knowledge to foster good corporate and social responsibility. He holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Mass Communication from Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ogun State, and a Higher Diploma in Public Relations from NIPR.

B.Sc - Mass Communication
Higher Diploma - Public Relations
